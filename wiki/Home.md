# Go-RomM-Sync Wiki

Welcome to the Go-RomM-Sync wiki! This project aims to provide a seamless synchronization and play experience for your RomM library using RetroArch.

## Navigation

- [Supported Platforms & Cores](Platforms.md) - Details on emulator compatibility, OS support, and RetroAchievements.
- [Setup Guide](../README.md) - Getting started with the application.

## About the Project
Go-RomM-Sync connects to your [RomM](https://github.com/romm-apps/romm) server, synchronizes your game library locally, and integrates with RetroArch for a premium "couch gaming" experience with full gamepad support.
